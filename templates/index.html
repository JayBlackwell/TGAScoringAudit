How It Works
  1. Connect - Enter your Golf Genius API key
  2. Select Season - Choose which season to audit
  3. Date Range - Specify the time period to analyze
4. Analyze - Automatically check all rounds for scoring issues
5. Results - Review flagged rounds and download reports
What We Check

The audit identifies rounds with potential scoring issues, specifically:

  • Incomplete front 9 scoring (holes 1-9)
  • Incomplete back 9 scoring (holes 10-18)
  • Missing or invalid score data
  • Data retrieval issues
